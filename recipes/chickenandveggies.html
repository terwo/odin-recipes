Chicken and Veggies

Description

The only home-cooked meal I ever made since coming to Japan. It's high in protein and nutrients, allowing me to somewhat reach my daily protein intake goals.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • A singular tomato
  • Chicken
  • Garlic
  • Virgin oil
  • A lot of spices
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Microwave that broccoli
  2. Heat up the stove, cut some garlic and put that in a oiled pan
  3. Cut up that chicken -- thinner is better
  4. To your heart's content, sprinkle as much spices on that thing as you want
  5. Cut up some tomatoes and pop that in too
  6. Mixey mixey yum yum
  7. Splash some milk onto the horror in front of you to make it creamier
  8. Voila!
